Pricing in INR

Our services are tailored to different categories, and pricing may vary based on the category you choose. Below are the details:

1. Job Seeker Category

₹48 - ₹499 (One-time Registration Fee) - This fee grants access to our platform, where you can browse job listings, apply for jobs, and receive career counseling services.

₹48 - ₹499 per year (Annual Renewal Fee) - After the first year, you will need to renew your access to the platform by paying the annual fee.

2. Job Provider Category

₹49 - ₹1,000 (One-time Registration Fee) - This fee allows companies to list job openings, manage applications, and access additional services for recruiting job seekers.

₹49 - ₹1,000 per year (Annual Renewal Fee) - After the first year, a renewal fee will be applicable to continue using the recruitment services.

Note: Pricing varies depending on the specific services and features you choose. All payments are in INR and are non-refundable once processed.
